Overview

Purpose

This is a hobby informational site. The first page will be the history of crochet and why I enjoy the hobby and my background in it. The second page will be about supplies needed to crochet, good places to get the and reputable brands. I would also like to try making a yarn yard calculator where you type in a project and it can estimate how many yards needed for that project.

Audience

My audience is beginner to intermediate crocheters. People looking for a new hobby or those looking for good recommendations of crocheting tools and supplies.

Dynamic elements

Yard calculator that allows you to put in a project or number of stitches and it calculates how many yards of yarn you need for the project.

Home page

Time to get 'hooked' on crochet! The History of Crochet The art of crochet originated in the mid 1820's. It was most popular in England, though it's actual origins are unknown as it has been claimed by several cultures. Crochet was developed as a craft in the 19th century. Crochet is performed by using a hook to create interlinked loops of thread that become yarn made works of art. The term "crochet" wasn't coined in England until 1837. It was found in a knitting book called 'The Ladies' Knitting and Netting Book'. It was mostly in english, but had one crochet pattern in it written in french. It only used the term once as it is the french word for the word 'hook'. Why crochet? Crochet can be a very relaxing activity. While it may take some time to get the knack of it, it is worth taking the time to learn. There is great satisfaction of turning a normal skein of yarn into anything from a snuggly children's plushie to a soft blanket to warm winter beanie. It is a hobby that is worth giving a try!
